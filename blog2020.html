A long break

Posted Wed July 27 2020

I have been continuing to work over the last four months, but this blog has lapsed. Rather than trying to fill the gaps, I'm going to start back with what I'm doing currently. This is what interests me enough to want to write about it.

In painting, I am trying to work with simple forms, which lets me experiment with their articulation in space. This has always been central to my work.

I have also started putting some serious figures into new work.

I've mostly skirted around the edge of figuration in the past. Perhaps this is about to change.

I'm also going back to some sculptural work, using various media to build forms. This will feed back into the painting.

First post for 2020

Posted Tues January 28 2020

I've wondered whether I should stop maintaining this blog, since I am still unsure of its purpose. But when I look back to earlier posts, it helps me to understand what I was concerned about at the time I wrote them.

Yes, I keep a personal painting diary, but the flavour of it is always a bit different from these public posts. I can't exactly explain how, but to me it feels generally more concise - which I imagine arises out of the subtle difference that I will put this out on the internet, whether anyone reads it or not.

Its a little bit like the difference between paintings that I've put in exhibitions (the public face of my art) and all the others that never get seen by anyone else. The odd thing is that these posts show exactly those works, which don't get out into the public. And since I don't have an intention (at present) to show work in exhibitions again, this becomes the central focus of my work. Or at least its documentation and at least some exposure to external scrutiny.
